## Overview
MiroFish is a multi-agent "swarm intelligence" prediction engine: it takes seed information (news, financial signals, even novel excerpts) and constructs a high-fidelity parallel simulation populated by thousands of independent agents with their own personalities and long-term memory, letting a user "rehearse the future" by observing how the simulation evolves.

## Problem It Solves
Traditional forecasting struggles to capture how collective, individual-level behavior produces emergent outcomes (public opinion shifts, market reactions). MiroFish approaches prediction as social simulation rather than pure statistical extrapolation, letting decision-makers test scenarios at zero real-world risk.

## Key Features / Use Cases
- **Graph-based world building** — seed extraction, entity/persona generation, and GraphRAG construction to build the simulated environment.
- **Dual-platform parallel simulation** — thousands of agents interacting concurrently with dynamic, temporal memory updates.
- **Deep interaction** — chat with individual simulated agents or a dedicated ReportAgent about the simulation's outcome.
- **Applications shown** — public opinion analysis, financial forecasting, and even fictional "what happens next" scenarios.
